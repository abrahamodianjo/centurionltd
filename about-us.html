Who we are

Fountain Dynamics

Fountain dynamics oil and gas company deals on diesel supplies in Abuja, Lagos and nearby states. We also facilitate the supply and export of diesel to international buyers around the world.

We provide a reliable, efficient and secure means to purchase quality diesel at the best rate.

We are well equipped with dedicated staff and state of the art equipment to meet your diesel needs.

Our stakeholders are diesel retailers, bulk distributors, government and private organizations, hospitals, churches, hotels & apartments, schools, manufacturing firms, and many others.

WHY CHOOSE US

We help preserve your generator life span because we supply NNPC standard diesel.

We supply diesel at a very competitive rate and we are open to negotiations. We also inform our customers when there is price fluctuation.

We ensure timely deliveries, handle small and bulk orders, we have accurate dispense meters and most importantly we deliver at your doorstep with our metered trucks/peddlers.

Our deliveries are top notch, so is our customer satisfaction which is one of our core values.

For your diesel supply needs, look no further, we are here to meet your diesel supply needs.
