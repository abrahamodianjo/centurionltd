Who we are

Centurion Group

Centurion is a Tanzania-based provider of integrated market access and logistics solutions founded in 2022. Our mission is to provide worldwide freight forwarding and customs clearance services with a key focus on the following industries: consumers, automotive, healthcare, and industrial commodities. We strive to offer the best service to our customers by providing innovative solutions to all their logistics queries using cutting-edge technology.

Our team at Centurion is made up of experienced professionals with a deep understanding of the logistics industry. We pride ourselves on our ability to tailor our services to each client's unique needs, ensuring that we provide them with the best possible solutions for their business.

At Centurion, we understand the importance of logistics in the success of any business. We believe that every client deserves exceptional service, and we go above and beyond to ensure that we deliver on that promise.

Centurion is a logistics company committed to providing innovative solutions and staying ahead of the curve in technology and logistics practices. With core values of integrity, professionalism, and excellence, Centurion aims to connect Africa and the world by offering quality products and services to improve individuals' lives. The company also values sustainability and community support, taking measures to reduce its carbon footprint and create opportunities for growth and development. Centurion is passionate about logistics and delivering exceptional service to clients across various industries. The company strives to make a positive impact on the world and is dedicated to building lasting relationships with its clients.

WHY CHOOSE US

We provide international shipping services to our clients, including air, sea, and land freight forwarding.

We offer customs clearance services to ensure our clients' shipments comply with regulations and reach their destination smoothly.

We provide logistics solutions to businesses in the consumer goods industry, helping them to transport and distribute their products effectively.

We specialize in providing logistics solutions to the automotive industry, including vehicle transport, parts distribution, and supply chain management.

We offer specialized logistics solutions to the healthcare industry, including medical equipment transport, temperature-controlled shipping, and pharmaceutical supply chain management.

We provide logistics solutions to businesses in the industrial commodities industry, including mining, oil and gas, and construction materials.

Choose Centurion for our exceptional logistics solutions, innovative technology, and commitment to sustainability and community engagement.
